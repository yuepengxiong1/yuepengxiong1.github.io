Hobbies & Interest

  • Video Game Modding: Messing around with game files is a good way to learn how does people in the top industry organize/do their files. Being able to use tools that reverse engineer a video game's propietrary format and making adding/editing content from the game breathes new air into the game. You are providing the updates to the game.
  • Motorsports: I enjoy watching cars go around in circles. I have been following Formula 1 since 2017 and have not stopped since. It's essentailly an engineering battle for the cars (yes the cars are not even) and a psycological battle between drivers. I also follow the more popular forms of races like the 24H of Le Mans, 24H of Nurburgring and the Indy 500
  • Car Detailing: Car detailing is a newer hobby for me but I do enjoy detailing a car. Its just nice to see your efforts on cleaning a car, polishing and waxing a car shine through on to the car itself.
  • Video Games:
      Rhythm Games: Provides a unique way to challenge yourself and see results get delivered by a day to day basis. Extremely motivating to keep playing when you see results and to push yourself. I usually play Etterna(Stepmania), osu!mania and Sound Voltex.

      Racing Games: I like cars and driving them. Sim racing is the best way to experience how a car drives and it is directly translatable to real life. Each car has its characteristics and makes it unique to drive from one another.

      Other: Besides those themes, I play casually, mainly with the friends I made playing rhythm games.
